Musiimenta Lilian

Finance Officer

Ensuring transparent financial management and fiscal accountability to support community-driven programs

Details

Role: Finance Officer

About Lilian

Musiimenta Lilian is a dedicated finance professional with experience in financial management, budgeting, and fiscal accountability. As the Finance Officer at CENCE Uganda, she plays a critical role in ensuring the organization's financial health and sustainability.

Lilian holds a Bachelor's Bachelors degree in Accounting with a specialization in Accounting from Kyambogo University and is a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) in Uganda. Her expertise includes financial reporting, grant management, budget preparation, and internal controls.

Responsibilities

  • Managing day-to-day financial operations and accounting processes
  • Preparing and monitoring organizational budgets
  • Ensuring compliance with donor requirements and Ugandan financial regulations
  • Producing accurate financial reports for management and stakeholders
  • Implementing and maintaining robust internal control systems
  • Coordinating with external auditors during annual audits
  • Training program staff on financial policies and procedures

Impact at CENCE Uganda

Since joining CENCE Uganda, Lilian has implemented improved financial systems that have increased transparency and accountability. Her meticulous approach to financial management has ensured that over 95% of donor funds are directly allocated to program activities, maximizing the impact on youth skilling initiatives.

Lilian's work has been instrumental in building donor confidence and securing continued funding for CENCE Uganda's programs, directly contributing to the organization's ability to empower more youth with vocational skills each year.

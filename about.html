About CENCE Uganda

Empowering Ugandan youth with skills for sustainable livelihoods through mindset transformation and practical training.

Our Story

The Shift Within: A movement to awaken youth potential.

After our studies, we felt lucky, not just proud. Going back home, we saw the reality many of our friends were facing. Bright, capable young people, stuck in a cycle of hopelessness, waiting for something to change. No jobs. No clear direction. No one to guide or invest in them. We realized that what separated us from them wasn't effort, it was opportunity.

That hit hard. And it pushed us to act. We came together as a small team of young professionals with one question: What if we became the support we once wished for? That's how our youth empowerment initiative (CENCE) was born in 2025, right there in the midwestern Ugandan district of Kibaale.

Challenges We Address

  • High unemployment and limited job skills
  • Youth waiting for jobs, support or rescue that may never come
  • Loss of confidence in their ability to shape their future
  • Few chances to start something of their own
  • Mindset captivity - the belief that success can only come from outside help

Our goal is to help youth become self-reliant, and confident in building their futures, all through mindset change and any related support. This isn't just about jobs, it's about empowerment from the inside out.

Our Mission

To empower youth with the mindset, skills, and support networks needed to break free from self-doubt, create sustainable livelihoods, and build a brighter future for themselves and their communities.

Our Vision

A Community where every young person has the skills, confidence, and opportunities to thrive economically and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

How We Unlock The Giant Within

1

Awaken Power

Through immersive Mindset Labs, we help youth break free from the narrative of "I can't" and build unwavering self-belief.

2

Build a Path

We combine practical skills training with micro-venture support and VSLA savings circles, enabling youth to earn from day one and grow sustainably.

3

Join a Tribe

No one walks alone. We facilitate peer support circles that foster collaboration, accountability, and a powerful sense of community.

Our Governance & Core Objectives

Our constitution establishes a clear and accountable structure, with a Board of Directors providing strategic oversight. This framework ensures that all our efforts are aligned with our core mission of empowering youth and transforming communities.

  • Provide Mindset Labs to foster self-belief
  • Equip youth with skills for micro-ventures
  • Facilitate peer support circles ("tribes")
  • Promote financial literacy via VSLA circles
  • Ensure effective data management and financial accountability

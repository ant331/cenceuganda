Gloria Nakalema

Project Coordinator - Implementation

Professional Profile

Implementation specialist with expertise in overseeing on-ground execution of programs and ensuring alignment with organizational objectives.

Gloria transforms strategic plans into actionable programs that directly benefit Ugandan youth. Her hands-on approach ensures that CENCE Uganda's initiatives are implemented effectively and efficiently at the community level.

Core Competencies

  • Program Implementation & Management
  • Community Engagement & Mobilization
  • Logistics & Operations Coordination
  • Performance Monitoring & Quality Assurance
  • Stakeholder Relationship Management
  • Problem-Solving & Adaptability
  • Team Leadership & Motivation
  • Strategic Planning & Execution

Key Responsibilities

  • Oversee day-to-day implementation of CENCE Uganda's programs
  • Coordinate with field staff to ensure program objectives are met
  • Monitor program progress and make adjustments as needed
  • Ensure all activities align with organizational values and goals
  • Report on program implementation progress to management
  • Develop and maintain strong community relationships

Professional Approach

Gloria believes that successful implementation requires both meticulous planning and the flexibility to adapt to changing circumstances. Her work ensures that CENCE Uganda's programs remain responsive to community needs while staying true to our mission.

She approaches each project with a focus on sustainable impact, ensuring that programs not only address immediate needs but also create lasting change in the communities we serve. Her dedication to excellence and attention to detail have been instrumental in the success of numerous CENCE Uganda initiatives.

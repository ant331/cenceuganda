C-Hearts for Children

Ensuring every child in remote Uganda has access to quality education and the opportunity to build a brighter future.

Education for Every Child

Breaking barriers to education in remote communities across Uganda.

In Uganda's most remote regions, thousands of children face significant barriers to accessing education. Distance, poverty, and lack of resources prevent many from ever setting foot in a classroom. C-Hearts for Children is our dedicated initiative to change this reality.

We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and build a better future through education. Our program focuses on removing the obstacles that keep children out of school and ensuring they have the support they need to succeed.

Challenges Facing Remote Children

  • Long distances to the nearest school
  • Lack of school supplies and uniforms
  • Poverty forcing children to work instead of study
  • Limited number of qualified teachers in remote areas
  • Inadequate school infrastructure
  • Cultural barriers & negative attitude towards education, especially for girls
  • Hunger and malnutrition affecting learning ability
  • Lack of early childhood education opportunities

Our Approach

1

Transportation Support

We provide bicycles and establish safe walking groups to help children overcome distance barriers to education.

2

Learning Materials

We supply textbooks, uniforms, and school supplies to ensure children have what they need to learn effectively.

3

School Nutrition

We implement school feeding programs to combat hunger and improve concentration in the classroom.

4

Teacher Support

We provide training and resources to teachers in remote schools to improve education quality.

5

Community Engagement

We work with parents and community leaders to change attitudes about education, especially for girls.

6

Scholarships

We provide scholarships for bright but disadvantaged students to continue their education beyond primary school.
